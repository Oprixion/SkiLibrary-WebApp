Find Your Fit
 Before you ski you must first find properly sized equipment in the library. This page is here to help with any sizing questions you may need answered or calculated. The below video covers much of what is displayed here.


Ski Pole sizing
 When looking for a correctly sized ski pole it is recommended for the pole's height to meet below or near the armpit. When securing your hand within the strap, take your hand and insert it through the strap loop from below. Then grip the pole. The ski pole may have a strap tightening feature which might be located on the top of the pole. Pull the straps on top until comfortable with the back of your hand. (Shown in video)

Ski boot sizing
 To find the correct ski boot size you can take your worn shoe size and find the european (uk) measurement. When trying on the boots there should ideally be some space for your foot to move. If the boots are too tight your feet might get cold.
