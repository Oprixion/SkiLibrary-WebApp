Library guidelines, eligibility & fines for borrowing equipment: …

Twenty full sets of classic technique Cross Country skis and four sets of snowshoes are available to Augustana Students, Faculty and Staff who have completed a one-time orientation session (this checklist or e-class session) and an annual waiver.
Loan period: 72 hours. Equipment must be returned to a Library staff member - cannot be left outside the Library or in the dropbox. Equipment must be clean and dry before returning to library. Microfibre towels available. Like most commercial ‘rentals’, you are responsible for returning all rental equipment in clean, dry, working condition. If the equipment you return is damaged, needs repair or requires cleaning, you’ll be responsible for those costs. You will be charged the full retail value for lost, stolen or destroyed equipment.
